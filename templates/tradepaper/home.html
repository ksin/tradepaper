trade-paper is coming soon!

trade-paper is a service created to connect you, a magazine owner, to other magazine owners, letting you trade your shelved magazines for other publications that you're after.

How does it work?

Add your magazines to your profile, search for a magazine you want, and send a trade request to the user with that magazine. Once both parties agree to a trade, we'll generate your shipping label and walk you through the shipping process to make sure everyone's happy.

When does it launch?

Everything should be up and ready around the end of September.
